    Health Insurance Network Types*

    Health insurance plans have different network types, sometimes referred to as “plan type”. There are many plan types; the most common are “health maintenance organization” (HMO), “preferred provider organization” (PPO), “exclusive provider organization” (EPO), and “point of service” (POS) plans. The different types are used as follows.

      HMO PPO EPO POS
    Has a network of doctors and/or hospitals
    Must use a network doctor and/or hospital    
    Need a referral to see a specialist   Varies* Varies*
    Covers care at an out-of-network doctor or hospital     Varies*

    *This is a general guideline. Please refer to a plan's details for specific coverage information.
